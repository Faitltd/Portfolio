Our Story

A Slice of New York in Centennial

Big Bill’s New York Pizza is a family-run restaurant built on the idea that everyone deserves a great slice and a warm welcome. We have been serving Centennial for more than 20 years, and we still treat every guest like family.

From our hand-tossed dough to our signature sauces, everything is crafted to bring the flavor of New York to Colorado.

2003

The First Slice

Big Bill’s opened its doors with a mission to serve authentic NY pizza with Colorado hospitality.
